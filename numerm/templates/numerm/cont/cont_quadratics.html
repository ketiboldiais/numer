Exercise. Solve: ${ 8x^2 - 3 = 10x }$

Solution
  • ${8x^x - 10x - 3 = 0}$
  • Subtracting ${10x}$ from both sides.
  • ${(2x + 3)(4x + 1) = 0}$
  • Factoring.
  • ${2x + 3 = 0}$
  • Solve for ${x.}$
  • ${2x = -3}$
  • ${x = \frac{-3}{2}}$
  • ${4x + 1 = 0}$
  • ${4x = -1}$
  • ${x = \frac{-1}{4}}$
  • ${x = \frac{-3}{2}, \frac{-1}{4}}$
  • Q.E.D.
