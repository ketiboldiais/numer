Preface

The following pages contain notes synthesized from my study of mathematics. I have a separate page, numerc, containing notes for computer science. I allocate what free time I can find each day learning something new in mathematics and computer science and record my findings here. Given my often hectic schedule, there may be errors or omissions. I try my best to correct these blunders during my week's end review, and during a broader cumulative review at month's end.

I am so fortunate to have the time and opportunity to study mathematics, when so many of us go through life without ever getting the chance. I hope the materials evidence some of the beauty in mathematics, and more importantly, provide others the opportunity.
