Assembly

In this module, we examine some basic ideas in assembly language. This will provide much of the foundations for later discussions. To do so, we will use the 6502 Assembly Language, as employed on the Atari 2600 platform. Such a simple platform is used for several reasons: (1) It allows us to avoid having to address the enormous stretches of complexity on modern devices, and (2) it captures most, if not all, of the most important ideas in assembly and computer architecture concisely.
