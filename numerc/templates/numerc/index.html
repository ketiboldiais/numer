Preface

The following pages contain notes synthesized from my computer science studies. The notes are constantly being revised and updated, so please excuse errors or omissions. I try allocating as much time as I can learning something new each day and reporting my findings here. I then do some reviewing at the end of each week where I address any errors and omissions from the week's findings.

Separately, I have another page, numerm, containing similar notes for mathematics. I hope at least one of these pages helps anyone else trying to understand and navigate the fascinating world of computer science and mathematics.
