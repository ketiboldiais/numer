Using this algorithm, in the worst case scenario we'd have to perform ${n}$ steps; the algorithm doesn't find a peak until the very end, ${i = n.}$ In the best-case scenario, the peak is the first element, ${i = 1.}$ We say that this algorithm has a complexity of ${\Theta (n).}$
